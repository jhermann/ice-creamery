# ⚒️ The Blade-Smith of the "Frozen Core": Master Resu

Deep within the sub-zero caves of the **Dairy Alps** lives **Master Resu**, the only blacksmith capable of forging weapons that don't shatter in the absolute zero of the Crystal Clan’s domain.

## The Forging of the X-Processor
Master Resu didn't use heat to forge Kuri-0's blade; he used **Cryo-Pressure**.
* **The Steel:** He used "Hardened Chrome-Alloy," a metal that actually gets tougher the colder it gets.
* **The Balance:** To ensure the 4-blade star was perfectly balanced, he had to forge it inside a rotating centrifuge. Even a 0.1mg weight difference would cause the blade to wobble and explode during a **High-RPM Spin**.

## 📖 The "Blade-Breaking" Prophecy
Master Resu warns every ninja who visits his forge of the **"Three Curses of the Machine"**:

1.  **The Curse of the Loose Nut:** If the central hub isn't tightened by the "Spindle-Key," the blade will fly off and destroy the ninja's own armor.
2.  **The Curse of the Liquid Core:** If you spin before the 24-hour mark, the center of the enemy will remain liquid, causing a "Splash-Back" that ruins your stealth.
3.  **The Curse of the Over-Fill:** If you try to process too much at once, the pressure will rise until the vault door itself blows off its hinges.



## 🦹 Villain File: Kobu (The Hump)

In the world of the **Crystal Clan**, there is no greater terror than **Kobu**. While other villains use cold or snow, Kobu uses the most dangerous weapon of all: **Uneven Expansion**. He is known as the "Blade-Breaker" because he is the only enemy Kuri-0 cannot simply spin through.

### 🎨 Visual Design (Anime Style)

- **The Body:** A massive, asymmetrical giant. One side of his body is smooth, but his right shoulder and back are covered in a jagged, rock-hard **"Mountain of Ice"** (The Hump).
- **The Texture:** His skin isn't smooth; it has the rough, dull texture of **Freezer Burn**. He is surrounded by a cold, dry mist that sucks the moisture out of the air.
- **The Armor:** He wears "Unleveled Plate," armor that is purposefully lumpy and tilted.
- **Color Palette:** Dirty Grey, Icy Translucent Blue, and "Warning Red" for his eyes.

### 📊 Vital Stats (The Science of Destruction)

| Attribute | Level | Specialization |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hardness** | MAX | Solid water ice with 0% sugar—harder than steel. |
| **Surface Tension** | Extreme | His body is never flat, making him impossible to "process." |
| **Blade Fatigue** | S-Tier | His presence causes mechanical stress to any weapon. |
| **Clan Rank** | High General | The Crystal Clan’s "Unstoppable Wall." |

### ⚔️ Combat Style: "The Axis-Wrecker"

Kobu doesn't dodge attacks. He waits for Kuri-0 to spin, and then he shifts his weight to destroy the Ninja's equipment.

1. **The Center-Point Strike:**
   - Kobu focuses all his mass into a single, raised peak. When a blade hits this "hump" at high speed, the vibration snaps the blade’s shaft instantly.
2. **Freezer-Burn Fog:**
   - He exhales a cloud of dry ice that removes all "Lube" (fats/oils) from the air, making Kuri-0’s joints grind and overheat.
3. **The "Never-Level" Earthquake:**
   - He slams the ground, causing the environment to tilt. This makes it impossible for the heroes to find a flat surface to "Freeze" their powers.

### 📖 Story Role: The Natural Enemy

Kobu is the ultimate test for our heroes. Kuri-0’s high-speed spins and Sofuto’s airy lightness mean nothing against a target that is **Solid, Uneven Ice**.

In the comic, Kuri-0 once tried to spin through Kobu without checking his "level," and his signature blade shattered into a thousand pieces. Now, Kuri-0 must find a way to "flatten" Kobu’s defenses before he can even start the fight.

> **Character Quote:** *"Spin all you want, little Ninja. My surface is jagged, my heart is solid, and your blades are... fragile. Welcome to the Deep Freeze!"*

### 🧪 The Science in the Script: The "Leveling" Law

Kobu represents the real-world danger of the Ninja Creami:

- **The Expansion Trap:** Kobu explains that when water freezes without enough sugar (**PAC**), it expands upward. This "Hump" is a concentrated point of stress.
- **Mechanical Failure:** In the comic, when Kobu appears, the "Warning Light" on Kuri-0’s visor blinks red. This is a nod to the "Install" light on the machine—if you aren't level, you aren't safe.
- **The Solution:** To defeat Kobu, the heroes have to use **Heat-Jitsu** (warm water or a scraper) to level him out before the final spin.



# 🥷 Character File: Kuri-0, the Creami-Shinobi

In a world where the "Crystal Clan" has turned every dessert into a block of flavorless ice, one ninja stands between the world and a bad texture. **Kuri-0** is a rogue alchemist who mastered the "Forbidden Spin" to bring silkiness back to the people.

## 🎨 Visual Design (Anime Style)

- **The Silhouette:** Slim and athletic, wearing a "Matte Carbon" ninja suit. His scarf is made of semi-transparent, glowing blue energy that looks like flowing cream.
- **The Headgear:** A sleek, white ceramic helmet with a spinning "Vortex Visor." When he calculates a recipe, digital numbers ($P_{AC}$ and $P_{OD}$) flicker across the glass.
- **The Blade:** He carries the **"Macro-Blade"** on his back. It’s a giant, curved spinning shuriken that doesn't cut—it *micro-mows* matter at 2,000 RPM.
- **Color Palette:** Pearl White, Obsidian Black, and "Electric Raspberry" pink accents.

## 📊 Vital Stats (The Science of a Shinobi)

| Attribute | Level | Specialization |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PAC Power** | S-Tier | Controls the "Anti-Freeze" energy to keep textures soft. |
| **POD Power** | A-Tier | Measures the "Power of Sweetness" to avoid flavor fatigue. |
| **Spin Speed** | 2,000 RPM | Necessary for breaking down stubborn ice crystals. |
| **Stealth** | High | Can turn a frozen brick into gelato in under 2 minutes. |

## ⚔️ Combat Style: "The Three Spins"
In the comic, Kuri doesn't just fight; he "processes" his enemies.

1.  **Style 1: The Sorbet Slash (LITE)**
     - A high-speed, precision move used for thin, icy opponents. It uses maximum RPM to turn water-based enemies into smooth slush.
2.  **Style 2: The Gelato Grind**
     - A dense, heavy-hitting style. Kuri slows down his movement to increase the pressure, creating a rich, heavy impact.
3.  **Style 3: The Mix-In Rain**
     - Kuri throws "Flavor Bombs" (cookies, candies, or nuts) into the air and uses his blade to perfectly distribute them into the environment.

## 📖 Story Hook: "The 24-Hour Wait"
Every comic hero has a weakness. For Kuri, it is the **"The Law of the Deep Freeze."** Before he can use his full power, he must enter a 24-hour meditation state inside a sub-zero chamber. If he is interrupted before the timer hits zero, his powers become "Crumbly" and "Unstable."

> **Character Quote:** *"A warrior without balance is just an ice cube. To find the silk, you must first embrace the freeze."*

## 🧪 The Science in the Script

Kuri uses real-world science as his "Magic System":

- **The PAC Gauge:** When he's losing a fight, it's because his $P_{AC}$ (Potere Anticongelante) is too low. He has to quickly inject "Sugar-Ether" or "Salt-Smoke" into the battlefield to lower the freezing point and regain his movement.
- **The POD Balance:** If he uses too much $P_{OD}$ (Potere Dolcificante), his enemies become "cloying" and slow him down. He must maintain the perfect 15-18% ratio to stay sharp.



# 🥷 Character File: Sofuto Sābu (Soft Swirl)

While Kuri-0 focuses on the "milling" and density, **Sofuto** is the master of **Volume**. He is a massive, bulky powerhouse who moves with the weightlessness of a cloud. He represents the "Lite Ice Cream" and "Creami Deluxe" functions—turning heavy ingredients into airy miracles.

## 🎨 Visual Design (Anime Style)

- **The Body:** He is a "Big Guy" character—broad shoulders and huge arms—but he doesn't walk; he bounces. His armor is rounded and puffy, resembling a pressurized marshmallow.
- **The Hair:** A massive, white, whipped-style afro that looks like a perfect soft-serve peak.
- **The Gear:** He wears **"Aero-Turbine" Gauntlets**. These huge gloves suck in air and inject it into anything he touches.
- **Color Palette:** Pastel Blue, Creamy White, and "Low-Cal" Mint Green.
- **The Vibe:** He is the "little brother" figure to Kuri-0—over-enthusiastic, loud, and always hungry.

## 📊 Vital Stats (The Science of Air)

| Attribute         | Level    | Specialization                                         |
| :---              | :---     | :---                                                   |
| **Overrun Power** | SSS-Tier | The ability to increase volume by adding air.          |
| **Protein Bond**  | A-Tier   | Uses "Whey-Shields" to keep the structure from collapsing. |
| **Density Control** | Low    | He struggles to stay grounded; he's too light!         |
| **Age**           | 16       | The "Gen-Z" prodigy of the Creami Dojo.                |

## ⚔️ Combat Style: "The Cloud-Weight Fist"

Sofuto’s fighting style is based on **Overrun** (the percentage of air in ice cream).

1. **The "Lite" Impact:**
   - Sofuto hits an enemy with a massive fist, but instead of crushing them, the impact "fluffs" them, sending them floating helplessly into the air.
2. **Pressure Expansion:**
   - He can expand his armor by injecting air, becoming a giant bouncy shield to protect Kuri-0 from "Crystal Shards."
3. **The Frothy Fog:**
   - He releases a mist of aerated dairy that blinds enemies. It’s like fighting inside a giant whipped cream canister.

## 📖 Story Role: The "Volume" Specialist

In the comic, Kuri-0 often makes things too "dense" and technical. Sofuto is there to remind him that **"Life needs more air!"** While Kuri-0 handles the frozen bricks, Sofuto is the expert on **Protein Fluff**. He can take a tiny amount of liquid and, through sheer "Spin-Jitsu," turn it into a meal that fills an entire bowl.

> **Character Quote:** *"Why be heavy when you can be huge? Watch me turn this pebble into a mountain of foam! Go, Lite Ice Cream Mode!"*

## 🧪 The Science in the Script: The Overrun Gauge

In their world, "The Void" is a desert of dense, icy rocks. Sofuto uses his knowledge of **Emulsifiers** to fix it:

- **The Protein Link:** Sofuto explains to the reader that air needs "anchors." He uses **Casein** and **Whey** (protein) as the "glue" that holds his air bubbles together.
- **The Calorie Paradox:** Because he is so "bulky" but made of air, he is actually the lightest member of the team. He can walk on water or snow without sinking, a literal "Lite" ninja.



# 🧊 The Realm: "The Freezer" (Reitō-ko)

In the world of the **Creami-Shinobi**, this isn't just a storage room—it’s a cold, silent purgatory where ingredients go to transform. It is the most dangerous environment for any ninja who hasn't mastered their **PAC** levels.

## 🌑 The Atmosphere: Dark & Oppressive
* **The Lighting:** A single, flickering industrial light hangs from the center of the ceiling. It casts long, sharp shadows that dance across the walls of frosted steel.
* **The Floor:** Covered in **"Pulvery Snow."** This isn't soft, winter snow; it’s dry, jagged ice dust—the remains of billions of failed emulsions and shattered sugar molecules.
* **The Air:** So cold it feels "dry." Every breath Kuri-0 takes comes out as a thick cloud of frozen mist. If you stand still for too long, the ice crystals will start to climb up your boots.

## 🚪 The Monolith Gate: The Flapper Door
The only way in or out is a **Massive Vault Door**.
* **The Feature:** In the center of the door is a **Single Heavy Flapper**.
* **The Function:** This is where "The Blade" enters the realm. When the flapper clicks open, a terrifying mechanical roar echoes through the room, and the sky seems to descend to "process" everything inside.
* **The Lore:** For a ninja, the Flapper is a ticking clock. Once it closes, you are trapped in total darkness for 24 hours of "Deep Freeze" meditation.

## 📦 The Storage Rows: The Frozen Pints
The walls are lined with high-tech shelving units holding glowing canisters.
* **The Waiting:** These canisters contain the "Essence of Flavor."
* **The Danger:** If a canister is stored too close to the central light, it develops **The Hump** (Kobu’s birthplace). If it’s stored in the corners, it becomes "The Brittle Sand."
* **The Silence:** The only sound is the low, ominous hum of the cooling fans, which sounds like the breathing of a sleeping giant.

## 📖 Story Hook: "The 24-Hour Sentence"
This is where the heroes must go to recharge. Kuri-0 and Sofuto enter **The Freezer** to undergo the **"Cryo-Sync."** * They must remain perfectly still under the central light.
* If they move, their internal $P_{AC}$ levels will fluctuate, causing them to emerge from the freezer as "Grainy" or "Icy" versions of themselves.

> **Kuri-0’s Warning:** *"Step carefully. The snow beneath your feet was once a warrior who rushed his freeze time. In this place, patience is the only weapon that doesn't shatter."*

## 🧪 The Science of the Setting
* **The Pulvery Snow:** This represents **Ice Recrystallization**. In the comic, if the temperature in "The Freezer" fluctuates even 1 degree, the snow grows into "Ice Spikes" that can pierce armor.
* **The Single Light:** This represents the **Heat Core**. It's the only place in the room where the temperature is stable enough to keep the "Mix-ins" from turning into rocks.



# 🏟️ The Battleground: "The Tub" (Enshinki-Jo)

In the center of the Great Pint-Vessels lies **The Tub**, a massive, cylindrical arena where the fate of texture is decided. It is the only place where the "Spin-Jitsu" can be performed at its maximum intensity.

## 🏛️ Architecture: The Cylinder of Combat
* **The Crystal Walls:** The arena is enclosed by towering, translucent walls made of **polycarbonate-diamond**. These walls are scratch-resistant but vibrate violently when the RPMs increase. Spectators watch from behind these glass layers as the battle blurs into a whirlwind.
* **The Floor (The Leveling Plane):** To a Creami-Shinobi, the floor is sacred. It is perfectly flat to prevent any mechanical imbalance.
* **The Dueling Pits:** The floor is notched with **concentrically placed rectangular pits**.
  * These pits serve as "anchors" for the combatants.
  * During a duel, the pits can retract or raise, forcing the ninjas to adjust their $P_{AC}$ levels to match the depth of the "freeze."
* **The Central Hub:** In the exact middle of the arena is a rotating pedestal where the **X-Processor Shuriken** is locked into place before a "Processing Dash."

## ⚔️ The Ritual of the Duel: "The Final Spin"
Duels in The Tub are not fought to the death, but to the **"Smoothness."**

1.  **The Drop:** The duelists are lowered into the cylindrical arena from the **Flapper Gate** above.
2.  **The Stabilization Phase:** Combatants must stay within the rectangular pits. If they step out before the "Freeze Timer" hits zero, they lose their structural integrity and become "Soupy."
3.  **The Spin:** Once the signal sounds, the entire floor begins to rotate. The ninjas must use their weapons (The Blade or The Blender) to carve through the icy obstacles generated by the Crystal Clan.

## ⛈️ Environmental Hazards: The "Icy Build-up"
The Tub is a dangerous place due to the **Centrifugal Force**:
* **Wall Cling:** As the arena spins, failed ingredients and defeated foes are flung against the crystal walls. This creates a "crust" of hard ice that the ninjas must avoid, or they will be "scraped" by the rotating blades.
* **The Bottom-Void:** If a ninja falls into the deepest concentric pit, they risk being trapped in the "Unprocessed Zone"—a dark place where the blades cannot reach, leaving them frozen forever.

## 🧪 The Science of the Arena
* **Concentric Pits:** These represent the different **Volumes** of the Ninja Creami containers (Pint vs. Deluxe). The deeper the pit, the more "torque" is required to escape.
* **The Scraper Effect:** In the comic, the "Walls" of the arena represent the outer edges of the pint where ice crystals often hide. The ninjas must "scrape the sides" to ensure the entire battlefield is smooth.



# ❄️ The Ninja Creami Parlor

The world of **The Ninja Creami Parlor** is a battleground of molecular physics. While the **Crystal Clan** seeks to lock the world in a permanent, gritty frost, the rogue alchemists of the **Creami-Dojo** use high-speed rotation and stabilization to fight back.

Here is the expanded technical lore of their arsenal and the world they inhabit.

## 🥷 The Heroes: Creami-Dojo

- [**Kuri-0**](characters/kuri.md) is a rogue alchemist who mastered the "Forbidden Spin" to bring silkiness back to the people.
- [**Sofuto**](characters/soft-swirl.md) is the master of **Volume**. He is a massive, bulky powerhouse who moves with the weightlessness of a cloud. He represents the "Lite Ice Cream" and "Creami Deluxe" functions—turning heavy ingredients into airy miracles.

## 🥶 The Villains: Crystal Clan

The "Crystal Clan" has turned every dessert into a block of flavorless ice, and only the Creami-Dojo stands between the world and a bad texture.

- [**Kobu**](characters/kobu.md) is the ultimate terror. While other villains use cold or snow, Kobu uses the most dangerous weapon of all: **Uneven Expansion**. He is known as the "Blade-Breaker" because he is the only enemy Kuri-0 cannot simply spin through.

## 🛠️ The Armory: Artifacts of the Spin

In this world, a ninja’s weapon is more than a tool—it is a "Processor." To defeat the Crystal Clan, one must be able to shave ice at the micron level.

### The X-Processor Shuriken (The Blade)
This is the legendary blade carried by **Kuri-0**. It is designed for high-velocity engagement within the "Pint Zone."

- **Design:** A perfect cross-star with four blades of equal length extending from a reinforced central hub.
- **The Dual-Edge System:**
  - **The Serrated Teeth:** Two opposite blades feature three razor-sharp "Milling Teeth." These are designed to shatter the $P_{AC}$ bonds of solid ice blocks.
  - **The Smoothing Edges:** The other two opposite blades are perfectly smooth, designed to "burnish" the cream and create the signature silk texture.
- **Orientation:** All edges face **counter-clockwise**. This allows the blade to "drill" into the frozen block, creating a vortex of flavor.
- **Combat Use:** Kuri-0 spins this blade at 2,000 RPM. The teeth hit first, breaking the enemy's defense, followed immediately by the smooth edge to "emulsify" the remains.

### The Emulsion Staff (The Blender)
A long-reaching tactical weapon used by **Sofuto Sābu** and apprentices for "Pre-Freeze" skirmishes.

* **Design:** A long, matte-black cylindrical shaft with a heavy, ergonomic power-grip at the top. At the business end sits a high-torque rotating micro-blade shielded by a protective bell.
* **Function:** This is the weapon of preparation. It is used to "bond" the liquids and solids before they enter **The Freezer**.
* **Combat Use:** The Staff is used to whip air into the environment, creating "Aero-Shields." If an enemy is too "lumpy," the Staff vibrates at high frequencies to break down fat globules until the mixture is perfectly homogenous.

### 💣 The Arsenal: "Mix-In" Grenades
In the Heat of Battle, Kuri-0 and Sofuto use tactical canisters known as **Flavor-Shells**. These are used to change the "physicality" of the battlefield.

| Grenade Type | Tactical Effect | Combat Visual |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Graham Grinder** | Friction increase. | Creates a gritty floor so the Crystal Clan can’t slide away. |
| **The Cocoa-Dust Cloud** | Obscuration. | A fine, dark powder that blinds enemies and absorbs light. |

## 🏔️ World Mechanics: The "Forbidden" Processing

The world is divided by the **Texture Wars**. Geography is defined by how things feel to the touch.

### The "Gritty Barrens"
A vast wasteland controlled by the Crystal Clan. The air is filled with "Ice Shards" that feel like sandpaper on the skin. Here, the $P_{AC}$ (Anti-Freeze Power) is zero. Everything is brittle, and nothing flows.

### The Great Pint-Vessels
The only safe havens for humanity. These are massive, cylindrical cities protected by thick, transparent walls. Inside these vessels, the atmosphere is pressurized and "leveled" to ensure the ground stays perfectly flat—preventing the birth of **The Hump**.

### The "Spin-Jitsu" Combat System
Ninjas don't just "slash"; they **Process**. A battle follows a strict 3-act structure:
1.  **The Emulsion:** Using the **Blender** to weaken the enemy's molecular structure.
2.  **The Deep Freeze:** A tactical retreat where the ninja lures the enemy into a cold environment to "set" their form.
3.  **The Final Spin:** Using the **X-Processor Shuriken** to achieve total victory—turning a hard, frozen foe into a defeated, creamy slush.
