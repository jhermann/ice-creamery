## Light Variant

Fat- and sugar-reduced variant that is also vegan.

### INGREDIENTS

**Prep**

  - _80ml_ Water (~85°C) • *alternative:* 120ml coconut milk 18.5%
  - _40g_ [Coconut Milk Powder 54% \[Green Essence\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#coconut-milk){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _0.40g_ [Glycerol Monostearate (E471) \[Bulk\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#glycerol-monostearate-gms-e471){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup> • *alternative:* lecithin
  - _45g_ [SweEX (Erythritol + Xylitol 3:2)](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#sweex-erythritol-xylitol-blend){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup> • *alternative:* 60g allulose or dextrose
  - _12 pcs_ Sweetener Tablets [LightSüß] • 1 tablet ≃ 4g sugar
  - _20g_ [Inulin \[Vit4ever\]](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#inulin){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup> • Sweetness = 8%; GI ~= 0

**Freezable**

  - _300ml_ Pineapple juice [REWE]

**Pourable**

  - _150g_ [White Rum 43 vol%](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#alcohol-ethanol){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>

### DIRECTIONS

 1. For the reconstituted coconut cream, mix the ‘prep’ ingredients (except the water) in a medium-sized bowl.
 1. Add the hot water and whisk until clump-free.
 1. Pour ‘freezable’ ingredients and the prepared coconut cream into an empty CREAMi Deluxe Tub up to the DRINKABLE FREEZE FILL line.
 2. With an immersion blender, mix until combined. Freeze for 24 hours, lid on.
 3. Remove tub from freezer and remove lid from tub.
 4. Add pour-in ingredients to the DRINKABLE POUR-IN line.
 5. Press FULL, then use the dial to select FROZEN DRINK and press dial to begin processing.
 6. Once processing is complete, transfer piña colada to a glass and serve immediately.
 7. **NOTE:** If you prefer a thinner frozen drink after processing, simply add 2–4 additional tablespoons
 of your desired pour-in (rum or juice), then select RE-SPIN.
