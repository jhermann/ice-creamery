## Classic Cocktail

### INGREDIENTS

**Freezable**

  - _70ml_ Cream 32% [REWE Beste Wahl]
  - _20g_ [White Rum 43 vol%](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#alcohol-ethanol){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>
  - _140ml_ Cream of Coconut 15% [Coco López] • 48% coconut milk
  - _270ml_ Pineapple juice [REWE]

**Pourable**

  - _130g_ [White Rum 43 vol%](/ice-creamery/info/ingredients/#alcohol-ethanol){target="_blank"}<sup>↗</sup>

### DIRECTIONS

 1. Pour ‘freezable’ ingredients into an empty CREAMi Deluxe Tub up to the DRINKABLE FREEZE FILL line.
 2. Stir mixture until combined. Freeze for 24 hours, lid on.
 3. Remove tub from freezer and remove lid from tub.
 4. Add pour-in ingredients to the DRINKABLE POUR-IN line.
 5. Press FULL, then use the dial to select FROZEN DRINK and press dial to begin processing.
 6. Once processing is complete, transfer piña colada to a glass and serve immediately.
 7. **NOTE:** If you prefer a thinner frozen drink after processing, simply add 2–4 additional tablespoons
 of your desired pour-in (rum or juice), then select RE-SPIN.
